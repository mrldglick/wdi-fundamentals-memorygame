Instructions

Concentration, also known as Match Match, Memory, Pelmanism, Shinkei-suijaku, Pexeso, or simply Pairs, is a card game in which all the cards are laid face down on a surface and two cards are flipped face u over each turn. The object of the game is to turn over pairs of matching cards.
